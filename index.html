I'm a web developer in central PA specializing in hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I work primarily with client-side optimization, making sure your site looks great in every browser and runs well on every device.

Whether you're building an Angular app or updating a ten year old CRM system, I can get you where you need to be. There's always a way to make things work, and I can help you find the cleanest, most efficient way to do it.

When I work on personal projects I like to be creative and have a bit of fun. Listed below are a few live demos, each with something behind-the-scenes that makes it a little interesting (at least to me) and unique.

Starfield (Source)
An HTML/CSS/jQuery animation of stars passing by, built to act as a lightweight background or sidebar animation. It's modular (easy to implement elsewhere), responsive, (works at any resolution), cross-browser, and cross-device.

3D Business Card (Source)
Another HTML/CSS/jQuery proof-of-concept for a rotating virtual bisuness card. I used a combination of folding and rotating animations to create the card itself as well as the flipping anitmaitons. I owe a big thanks to the awesome JavaScript library transit.js made my code much more concise and legible, while keeping the animations cross-browser compatible and mobile-friendly using a single method.
